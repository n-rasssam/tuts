Questionnaire Briefing

  • General Information(Name of the company | contact person | Address | Telefon | Email)
  • What are the goals of the project?
  • What tasks should fulfill your website?
  • Who is the target group? Interests, needs, skills, age, school education? Language?
  • What is the content of the site?
  • What features should have the site?
  • Is there a coporate design / coporate identity or design guidelines?
  • Are there any previous multi-media productions? Well, were you satisfied with that?
  • Are there (positive or negative) role models for production?
  • Is there a firm advertising agency?
  • What is the promotion for the website?
  • What is the unique feature - in the marketing language "unique selling proposition" (USP)? (What makes the client different from its competitors?)
  • What are the contents (text, images, etc.)? Is the quality sufficient and does the client have the rights to the materials?
  • Is there already a domain?
  • What are the technical conditions? Is there already a server on which the site should be loaded? Should a database be accessed?
  • What is the time frame? When should the site be online?
  • How should the site be maintained and updated? What contents are affected and to which extent?

Try to the following Informations:

  • How does look like the web appearance of the main competitors? Do they have functions that go beyond what your potential client proposes? What is good, what bad?
  • How does the company stand in the market? Is it a small challenger or market leader?
  • How does the company represent to the outside?
  • What is the meaning of the web for the company? A florist, for example, will attach a very different importance to the Web than an IT company?
